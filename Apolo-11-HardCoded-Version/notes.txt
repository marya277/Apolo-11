Missions short names
1- OrbitOne = ORBONE
2- ColonyMoon = CLNM
3- VacMars = VCMS
4- GalaxyTwo = GXTO
5- Unknow: UNKW

Creating the path to the devices folder on
the simulation_engine.py file:
DEVICES_FOLDER_PATH = Path(__file__).parent.parent / "data" / "devices"

Los estados posibles para cada dispositivo son: excellent (excelente), good
(bueno), warning (advertencia), faulty (defectuoso), killed (inoperable) y
unknown (desconocido).
